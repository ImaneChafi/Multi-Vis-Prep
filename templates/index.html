Surfaces are rendered semi-transparent so overlapping structures stay visible. Overlap @0.5 mm is the main quality metric (fraction of IOS points within 0.5 mm of a tooth surface). A flipped or wrong alignment scores low here even if coverage looks high. RMSD is the root-mean-square residual over matched points. The 4×4 transform (IOS→CBCT) and its inverse are saved under outputs/<scan-name>/.
